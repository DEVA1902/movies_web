🎬 Mankatha (2011) – Full Movie Explanation

1. Intro – Police & Criminals

The movie opens with Assistant Commissioner of Police (ACP) Prithviraj investigating a massive illegal cricket betting network in Mumbai. The betting mafia, run by Arumuga Chettiyar, handles crores of black money. At the same time, a group of youngsters — Sumanth, Ganesh, Mahat, and Prem — plan to rob ₹500 crores of betting money being transported by Chettiyar’s gang.

2. Vinayak Mahadevan Enters

We meet Vinayak Mahadevan (Ajith Kumar) — a suspended police officer. He is stylish, arrogant, witty, and completely selfish.

He doesn’t care about morals or friendship.

He’s suspended for misusing his power but still has contacts in the police.

He meets Trisha (his girlfriend), daughter of a police commissioner.

By chance, Vinayak comes across the youngsters’ plan to loot the betting money. Seeing a golden opportunity, he forces himself into the gang.

3. The Big Heist

The group carefully executes the heist. They successfully steal ₹500 crores meant for Chettiyar. But the gang now faces the real problem — how to share the money and escape. Vinayak, being much more cunning, manipulates the group. He promises them equal share, but in his mind, he wants everything for himself.

4. Betrayal Begins

After the robbery, Vinayak double-crosses the team: He kills Prem. He traps Sumanth and Ganesh in dangerous situations. He cleverly hides the money in a van and pretends like it’s lost. The gang members start turning against each other, not realizing Vinayak is pulling the strings.

5. ACP Prithviraj on the Hunt

Meanwhile, ACP Prithviraj is hot on their trail. He slowly discovers that a police insider is involved. He suspects Vinayak, but since Vinayak is smart and manipulative, he evades capture multiple times.

6. Cat & Mouse Game

The second half is a rollercoaster of: Vinayak killing one teammate after another. Double-crossing Chettiyar’s men. Outsmarting the police at every turn. Every time you think Vinayak is trapped, he plays a new trick to escape.

7. Final Twist

In the climax, when the dust settles: Vinayak manages to get hold of the entire ₹500 crores. But just as he celebrates, ACP Prithviraj corners him. In a shocking twist, Prithviraj shoots Vinayak. Vinayak dies with his trademark devilish smile, proving he remained unapologetically selfish till the very end.

⚡ Themes & Style

  • Anti-hero character: Unlike usual Tamil heroes, Ajith plays a negative, greedy, cunning role.

  • Greed & Betrayal: Everyone wants the money, but Vinayak is the greediest.

  • Stylish Action: Car chases, gunfights, and unexpected betrayals keep the movie thrilling.

  • Venkat Prabhu’s “Game”: The tagline “It’s a game” reflects the unpredictable screenplay.

🌟 Why Fans Loved It

  • Ajith’s massive stylish performance as a ruthless anti-hero.

  • Yuvan Shankar Raja’s electrifying BGM & songs (e.g. Vilayadu Mankatha).

  • Non-stop twists, betrayals, and suspense.

  • A rare Tamil film where the hero is truly grey-shaded, selfish, and unapologetic.
