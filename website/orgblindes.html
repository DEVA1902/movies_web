Peaky Blinders — The Full Saga (Seasons 1–6)

A cinematic, single-page overview of the Shelby family’s rise, betrayals, and battles — ending where the upcoming film begins.

Quick Facts

  • Timeline: 1919 → 1939
  • Locale: Birmingham, London, & beyond
  • Theme: Power, loyalty, redemption
  • Icon: Razor in the cap 🎩

Act 1: Rise of the Peaky Blinders (1919 — Season 1)

Post–World War I Birmingham. Ambition ignites.

Thomas Shelby, a decorated war veteran, returns to a broken Birmingham and leads the Peaky Blinders, a razor-cap gang with ambitions beyond the streets. A seized cache of stolen guns draws the wrath of Inspector Campbell, sent to crush the gangs. Tommy falls for Grace Burgess, not knowing she’s undercover. By season’s end, Tommy outmaneuvers Campbell, expands his empire, and loses Grace — for now.

Key Beats
  • Stolen government guns spark a citywide manhunt.
  • Grace’s double life strains her loyalties.
  • Tommy asserts vision: from gangland to legitimate power.

Act 2: Blood & Business (1921 — Season 2)

The London expansion. New enemies, uneasy allies.

The Shelbys push into London, colliding with the Sabini syndicate and striking volatile deals with Alfie Solomons. Grace returns, married yet tethered to Tommy. After brutal turf wars and political manipulation, Tommy defeats Sabini but discovers the government intends to use him for darker tasks.

Key Beats
  • Alfie’s betrayals and alliances become a recurring knife’s edge.
  • Grace’s return complicates power and emotion.
  • Tommy’s entanglement with state operations begins.

Act 3: Family & Betrayal (1924 — Season 3)

A wedding, a murder, and a plunge into conspiracy.

Tommy marries Grace, but happiness shatters when she’s assassinated at a charity event. Grief drives Tommy into high-stakes deals with Russian aristocrats and the Church. He gains wealth and influence yet pushes his family to a breaking point.

Key Beats
  • The Shelby family’s trust begins to fracture.
  • Tommy’s pursuit of power eclipses his humanity.
  • Enemies multiply in the shadows of state and church.

Act 4: The Politics of War (1926 — Season 4)

Vendetta descends from across the ocean.

Luca Changretta, a Sicilian mobster, arrives to annihilate the Shelbys after their betrayal of his family. Through brutal confrontations and a precarious pact with Alfie Solomons, Tommy outwits Luca. In a shocking evolution, Tommy becomes a Member of Parliament, stepping from crime to politics.

Key Beats
  • Near-destruction of the family forces new tactics.
  • Alfie’s double-crossing peaks — and pivots.
  • Tommy trades the street for the state.

Act 5: The Shadow of Fascism (1929 — Season 5)

The crash, the visions, and Mosley’s rise.

The world buckles under economic collapse. As an MP, Tommy fights inner torment and recurring visions of Grace. Enter Oswald Mosley, spearheading British fascism. Tommy pretends to cooperate while plotting Mosley’s assassination. Michael Gray returns from America, challenging Tommy’s rule. The plot fails due to betrayal within the ranks, and Tommy, crushed, stares down the abyss of his own mind.

Key Beats
  • The family splinters between old guard and new ambition.
  • Mental trauma becomes Tommy’s fiercest foe.
  • The failed assassination resets the war with Mosley.

Act 6: The End of the Road (1933 — Season 6)

Loss, lies, and a lonely ride into the future.

Polly Gray is killed by the IRA, devastating the Shelbys. Michael, vowing revenge, moves against Tommy and pays with his life. Tommy is told he’s terminally ill — a lie engineered by enemies linked to Mosley. Realizing he’s been spared, Tommy burns his old life and disappears, alive and unbound.

Key Beats
  • Polly’s death leaves a permanent scar.
  • Michael vs. Tommy ends in finality.
  • The illness ruse reveals a larger conspiracy.

Where the Movie Begins (1939 — World War II)

The table is set: Tommy Shelby vs. British fascism.

The series closes with Tommy alive and in the wind. As Europe edges into war, the next chapter points toward a confrontation with Oswald Mosley, renewed family schisms, and one last reckoning for the Peaky Blinders’ legacy.

Predicted Film Trajectory (Fan Outline)
  • Opening: 1939. Tommy resurfaces, sharper than ever.
  • Gathering Storm: Family divided; fascist power ascends.
  • Blood & Brotherhood: Allies old and new; betrayal from within.
  • War with the Devil: Open conflict as WWII begins; a showdown with Mosley.
  • Finale: A choice between power and redemption; a curtain call on the Shelby name.

Note on the Movie

The official Peaky Blinders film has not been released at the time this page was written. The “Predicted Film Trajectory” is a fan-made outline based on Season 6 and creator hints, and is not the confirmed plot.
