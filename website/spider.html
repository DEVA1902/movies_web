🕷️ Spider-Man: No Way Home – Full Story

Opening

  • The movie picks up right after Spider-Man: Far From Home.

  • The spell becomes unstable and cracks the multiverse open.

Act 1 – Seeking Help

  • Desperate, Peter goes to Doctor Strange to ask him to cast a spell that makes everyone forget he’s Spider-Man.

  • During the spell, Peter keeps interrupting, asking Strange to exclude MJ, Ned, and Aunt May from forgetting.

Act 2 – Villains Arrive

  • Strange captures Doctor Octopus on a bridge. Otto realizes Peter isn’t his Peter Parker.

  • Strange explains villains from other universes who knew Spider-Man’s identity were pulled in.

  • The villains include:

  • Green Goblin (Norman Osborn)

  • Electro (Max Dillon)

  • Sandman (Flint Marko)

  • Lizard (Curt Connors)

  • Doctor Octopus (Otto Octavius)

  • Strange wants to send them back to their universes, where most of them die fighting Spider-Man.

Act 3 – Peter’s Choice

  • Peter, guided by Aunt May, believes they deserve a second chance.

  • He steals Strange’s magical cube (which could send them back) and traps Strange in the Mirror Dimension. Peter tries to “cure” each villain using Stark tech and science. He fixes Doc Ock’s inhibitor chip, restoring Otto’s good side. But Green Goblin’s evil side takes control again, leading to betrayal. Tragedy

  • In a brutal fight, the Goblin kills Aunt May, delivering the iconic line:

  • “With great power, there must also come great responsibility.”

Act 4 – The Multiverse Expands

  • Ned and MJ, trying to find Peter, accidentally open portals with Strange’s sling ring.

  • From those portals step in… Tobey Maguire’s Spider-Man (Spider-Man trilogy, 2002-07) and Andrew Garfield’s Spider-Man (Amazing Spider-Man, 2012-14).

  • They find Tom Holland’s Peter, comfort him, and remind him of their own losses (Uncle Ben, Gwen Stacy).

  • Together, the three Spider-Men team up to cure the villains.

Final Battle – Statue of Liberty

  • The Spider-Men lure the villains to the Statue of Liberty.

  • With teamwork, they cure Sandman, Lizard, and Electro.

  • Doc Ock helps them fight.

  • Andrew Garfield’s Spider-Man redeems himself by saving MJ (after failing to save Gwen in his universe).

Climax

  • The multiverse begins tearing apart as more villains start leaking in.

  • Doctor Strange tries to hold it, but it’s breaking.

  • Peter realizes the only solution: make everyone forget Peter Parker exists — not just Spider-Man’s identity.

  • Strange cass the spell. Everyone forgets Peter Parker completely, including MJ and Ned.

Ending

Peter visits MJ and Ned but doesn’t reveal his identity, letting them live safe lives. He makes a new suit and continues as Spider-Man, completely alone but stronger.
